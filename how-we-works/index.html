How DaanPitara Works

From Visibility to Viability — We’ve Got You Covered

DaanPitara is not just a platform. It’s your digital growth engine, your fundraising GPS, and your entry point to the CSR ecosystem. Here's how it works:

🟢 Step 1: List Your NGO for Free

📍 Start by registering your NGO on our platform: daanpitara.org/ngo/registration

  • A verified spot on India’s first NGO Map Mosaic
  • A dedicated digital profile page (acts as your website!)
  • Increased visibility to donors, CSR companies & the public

🟡 Step 2: Get Digitally Ready in 7–10 Days

  • ✅ Social Media setup & branding
  • ✅ Google My Business listing
  • ✅ Basic website/microsite via your profile
  • ✅ SEO foundation to boost discoverability

🟠 Step 3: Activate Google Ad Grants (Worth ₹8L/month)

  • 📈 Get more website visitors
  • 💳 Drive donations online
  • 📞 Get volunteer & donor inquiries
  • 📣 Amplify awareness campaigns

🔵 Step 4: Launch Smart Fundraising Campaigns

  • 🎥 Emotional videos & storytelling
  • 🎨 Creative posters, reels & designs
  • 📲 WhatsApp & email fundraising automation
  • 📢 Facebook + Google ad campaigns
  • 💬 Donor funnel messaging

🟣 Step 5: Get CSR-Ready (Compliance + Proposal)

  • 📄 Proposal creation & impact documents
  • 📌 CSR Form 1, 80G, 12A, FCRA evaluation
  • 🗃️ Donor pitch decks & success stories
  • 🤝 CSR matchmaking with real partners

🟤 Step 6: Postpaid, Performance-Based Billing

You only pay for services like SEO, Google Ads setup, and CSR campaign management after visible progress or from raised donations.

💡 We also offer commission-based fundraising — no big upfront budgets needed.

🔴 Step 7: 100 Days = ₹1 Crore Digital Fundraising Engine

  • 🔥 Organic growth via Google & social media
  • 📊 Targeted fundraising & CSR campaigns
  • 💰 High-value donors & CSR partners
  • 📈 Scale visibility, credibility, and donations

🧩 Bonus: All-in-One Dashboard (Coming Soon)

  • ✅ Track leads, donors, and campaign traffic
  • ✅ Monitor campaign performance in real-time
  • ✅ Get alerts for grants & CSR opportunities

🔔 TL;DR – Why DaanPitara?

  • 🎯 Digital strategist
  • 📢 Fundraising engine
  • 🤝 CSR connector
  • 🛠️ Tech partner
  • 📈 Growth accelerator
